Online Mandarin Course

Product Design

Embark on an immersive Mandarin learning journey with Chestnut Academy's groundbreaking course, now available on Udemy. As the lead learning consultant for this project, I've integrated innovative storytelling and context-based learning methodologies to create a uniquely engaging experience. This course, the first in our practical Mandarin series, is designed to build a solid foundation for both travel in China and deeper cultural understanding.

By leveraging the power of narrative, we've transformed traditional language instruction into an adventure that resonates with both internal and external clients. Students will find themselves effortlessly absorbing Mandarin through carefully crafted scenarios and cultural contexts, making the learning process both effective and enjoyable.

Key features of this course include:

  • Immersive storytelling that brings Mandarin to life
  • Real-world contexts that prepare you for actual travel experiences
  • Cultural insights woven seamlessly into language lessons
  • Engaging exercises designed to reinforce learning through practical application
