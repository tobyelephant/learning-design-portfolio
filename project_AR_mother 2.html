AR storytelling

Overview

This Augmented Reality Application is to provide a new and immersive way of storytelling. Instead of only reading text and images, readers can now watch 3D animations that tell the story by using this app and scanning the pictures in the book. The story is about a baby sparrow that encounters three animals and finds his mother in the end with help from Mr. Dandelion.

Features

We created a children's story booklet that has text and images. Readers need to download our application and then scan the image to trigger the animation playing on each page. There are 12 scenes that readers can interact with while reading the story.

Technology

Augmented Reality; Unity 3D; ARcore; Vuforia SDK
