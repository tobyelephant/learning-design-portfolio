VR Game: Hotel Training Simulation

Overview

This project is to develop a training simulation for hotel front desk training. Trainees play as a hotel front desk going through the guest check-in process. In this way, students in the hotel industry can gain hands-on experience easily through practicing in virtual reality simulations.

By pushing different menus and buttons in the game scene, players can interact with our virtual guest Calvin. The booking list on the left-side monitor displays the guest names that are about to arrive. The training instructions on the right-side monitor guide players to finish this training session.
