Fruit Garden Virtual Tour

Overview

This VR experience is to provide a virtual tour of the Botanic Garden in Montreal. All 360 images are taken with the 360 camera and post-edited in Adobe Premier Pro and After Effects. The tour is made in Adobe Captivate and hosted on Adobe e-learning.

Features

Viewers can choose different viewpoints on this tour and enjoy a 360-degree view of each spot. There is some information and pictures of the plants to read by only clicking the icon. Forward and Back icons guide users to move to the next scene or return to the previous location.
